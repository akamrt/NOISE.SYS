## 🧵 Post 6 — NON-DESTRUCTIVE DEEP DIVE (Day 7)
*For technical followers*

> Here's why non-destructive noise matters 🧵
>
> Traditional approach: you add noise to your camera rig.
> It *replaces* your keyframes.
> Want to adjust later? Start over.
>
> NOISE.SYS approach:
> It adds a plusMinusAverage node network on top.
> Your original animation stays intact.
> Toggle it off with one button.
>
> And when you're happy: [BAKE TO LAYER]
> Commits cleanly to an additive AnimLayer.
> Your timeline stays clean.
>
> That's the workflow.
